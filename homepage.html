About

EyeCatcher is an international photography agency owned by the genius Armand. It had its humble beginnings in a rural area in Zamboanga City, servicing students and professionals during the late 70's. It gained traction when a group of American researchers came in to inquire about a possible camera that was capable of capturing micro objects, for which he gladly volunteered to not just lend them, but to personally take shots of the objects without even asking for anything. Little did Armand know that the group of American researchers were working for National Geographic, which at that time was only starting out. The photos came in and they were practically in awe at the beauty of the shots that Armand took. He was immediately recommended to National Geographic by these researchers.

His reputation for having one of the best eye for angle and color spread at the international scene like wildfire, with major international broadcasting networks hiring him and his photography agency, the EyeCatcher. It wasn't long before he was able to break through Hollywood, which to most people, is nothing but a dream.

He now resides in a city in Manila, Philippines, along with his loving and supportive wife and six children. Business is booming and EyeCatcher remains to be on top of the game.
